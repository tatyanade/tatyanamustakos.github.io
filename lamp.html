Lamp

 

Create a lamp with cardboard, chipboard, styrofoam, or paper, and no binding agents.

 

Product Description:

 

This cup holder holds not only cups, but it also holds as much existential crisis and disdain as any piece of acrylic can. Standing on all fours, this cup holder will hold whatever you want

 

 

 

 

Process:

 

 

Below are some sketchbook photos. I designed the sketch by looking at images, as seen on my moodboards below

 

Made my lamp with three main parts: the base, the 'trunk', which has the light hang off of it, and the support, which braces the trunk. After putting the light together, I wanted to create a sort of lightbulb to put my LED strip in, and so I made an origami cube which hangs on the wire and defuses the red and blue into a nice purple color, which ads ambiance.

 

I sketched the parts on paper then created vectors in illustrator which I later lasercut out of cardboard
