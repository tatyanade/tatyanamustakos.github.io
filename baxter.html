Feeding with Baxter
Main site
Collaborative project with Kearnie Lin, Lingdong Huang, and Vicki Long. Programming a Baxter robot to be able to feed people through facial recognition, object recognition. Done under Professor Akihiko Yamaguchi for the class 16-264 Humanoids, at the Robotics Department in Carnegie Mellon University.
